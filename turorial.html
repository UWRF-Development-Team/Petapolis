Petapolis!

How to play

click the red button to gain flowers

How to convert

click the flower to money icon in the bottom right of the screen

How to buy upgrades

click on the trowel or gardener buttons to buy upgrades to help you gain more flowers.

  • the trowel gives you more flowers per click
  • the gardener adds flowers per second based on the amount of gardeners bought

Prestige

to show of your web gardening skills, you can prestige! after you have 1 million flowers, you can click the prestige button which will set you back to 0. This gives you a chance to feel what it was like to be a noob again and start another gardening journey!
